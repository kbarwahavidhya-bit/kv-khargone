Aurelia Academy offers a hybrid global curriculum. For early years and primary levels, we follow the International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme (IB PYP). Middle years follow the IB MYP, while high school features both advanced pre-university classes and the highly respected IB Diploma Programme.
Yes. Aurelia Academy offers merit-based academic scholarships, excellence scholarships in visual and performing arts, and financial need-based student assistance grants. Scholarships cover up to 50% of tuition fees. Apply during Step 2 of enrollment.
Yes, we maintain a fleet of modern, air-conditioned school buses equipped with GPS tracking and safety wardens. Routes cover all primary residential zones within a 25-mile radius of the school. Bus registration is billed annually.
