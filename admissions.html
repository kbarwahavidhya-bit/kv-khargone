Our Admission Process

We keep our admission procedure clear and student-focused to ensure a smooth transition for your family. Follow these four steps to secure enrollment.

Step 1

Online Inquiry

Complete our admissions inquiry form on the contact page. Provide basic academic history and student details so we can understand your objectives.

Step 2

Campus Visit & Tour

Book a tour of our smart campus. Meet with a counselor, inspect the science labs, arts studio, and library, and learn about curriculum levels.

Step 3

Student Assessment

Prospective students complete a basic placement assessment in English literacy, mathematics, and critical analysis to determine level assignment.

Step 4

Enrollment Offer

Successful candidates receive a formal admission package. Confirm seat placement by submitting registration documentation and the enrollment fee.
