Global Ethos, Indian Roots

Nurturing the Whole Child

At Aurelia Academy, we synthesis international pedagogy with deep-rooted values of Indian heritage. We cultivate character, respect, mindfulness, and collaborative fellowship through our House System, classical arts, daily yoga assemblies, and nutritious regional meals.

Healthy Competition & Fellowship

The House System

Every Aurelian belongs to one of four Houses named after India's historic rivers, driving athletic, academic, and creative collaborations.

G

Ganga House

"Purity & Unstoppable Progress"

Symbolizing wisdom and steady determination, Ganga House fosters academic dedication and resilience in sport.

Y

Yamuna House

"Devotion, Unity & Flow"

Representing community coordination and harmony, Yamuna House dominates school debates and creative arts.

N

Narmada House

"Inner Strength & Vitality"

Reflecting physical vigor and athletic power, Narmada House holds our school sports championships.

K

Kaveri House

"Grace, Abundance & Prosperity"

Highlighting kindness and cultural appreciation, Kaveri House leads social welfare and community drives.

Sanskriti Program

Traditional Heritage & Arts

Our co-curricular activities incorporate traditional practices to enrich analytical logic, focus, and cultural appreciation.

🧘

Yoga & Mindfulness

Our school day begins with pranayama, surya namaskars, and silent meditation, promoting emotional self-regulation and healthy physical posture.

🪕

Classical Performing Arts

Students select modules in Hindustani/Carnatic vocals, Tabla, Sitar, flute, Kathak, or Bharatanatyam, guided by classical experts.

🧮

Vedic Maths & Sanskrit

Introductory Sanskrit builds linguistic structure and reading roots, while ancient Vedic mathematics speeds up mental arithmetic and spatial reasoning.
