Educational Philosophy

Nurturing Intellect and Character

Our curriculum is designed to stimulate critical inquiry, creativity, and deep conceptual understanding. Combining world-recognized educational standards (IB & IGCSE frameworks) with project-based collaborative learning, we prepare students to adapt and excel in an ever-evolving world.

Early Years Programme (Ages 3-5)

Our early years curriculum centers on structured play, discovery-based activities, and social-emotional development. We cultivate natural curiosity in a safe, stimulating bilingual environment.

  • Inquiry and play-based learning approach
  • Immersive language arts and communication
  • Sensory exploration & fine motor development
  • Strong foundation of emotional intelligence

Primary School Programme (Ages 6-10)

During the primary years, we emphasize core skills in language arts, mathematics, and inquiry-led science. Students develop study habits, collaboration skills, and global awareness.

  • IB Primary Years Programme (PYP) structure
  • Integrated science and social studies inquiry
  • Comprehensive Singapore-style Mathematics
  • Vibrant visual arts and music programs

Middle School Programme (Ages 11-13)

Middle school bridges childhood learning with high school rigor. Specialist teachers lead distinct subjects, encouraging independent research and critical analysis.

  • Rigorous subject-specific expert instruction
  • Advanced STEM and robotics laboratories
  • World languages options (Spanish, French, Mandarin)
  • Comprehensive physical education and wellness programs

High School Programme (Ages 14-18)

High school preparation focuses on university admission, analytical thinking, and global awareness. Students complete the prestigious IB Diploma or pre-university AP credentials.

  • International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme
  • Pre-college university guidance counseling
  • Advanced research essays and theory of knowledge
  • Extensive community service projects (CAS)
State-of-the-Art Environment

World-Class Facilities

Aurelia's modern architecture and smart-campus amenities support advanced learning, scientific discovery, and creative arts.

Science & STEM Labs

Equipped with collegiate-grade chemistry, biology, and physics apparatus alongside 3D printers, VR equipment, and robotics coding kits.

The Academic Library

Our two-story library houses 30,000+ volumes, private study carrels, research database terminals, and collaborate discussion pods.

Creative Arts Center

A dedicated complex containing soundproof music practice rooms, a digital graphics studio, ceramics pottery wheels, and a drama theatre.
