Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, balanced, and premium bilingual education program that fosters critical questioning, sparks active collaboration, builds emotional intelligence, and equips students to lead lives of genuine purpose and global impact.

Our Vision

To remain at the global forefront of educational innovation, cultivating an inclusive learning ecosystem where diverse student potentials are maximized, character is forged, and graduates emerge as empathetic leaders of tomorrow.

Our History

A Brief History of Aurelia Academy

Founded in 2008, Aurelia Academy began with a single vision: to create a premium school that balances classical academic fundamentals with cutting-edge scientific exploration and creative arts. What started with just 120 primary school pupils quickly grew into one of the region's top-tier secondary schools.

In 2018, we launched our state-of-the-art smart campus featuring advanced robotics laboratories, collegiate-level sports structures, and the double-height library. Today, Aurelia accommodates over 1,500 students representing 40+ nationalities, maintaining our commitment to individual student progress.
